# Reflection & Learning Loop Contract

## Core Purpose
Audit closed project milestones, analyze deviations, and extract lessons learned.

## Quality Gates
- **Entry Preconditions**: Completed milestone reports and planning metrics
- **Required Context**: Original plan and registry logs
- **Execution Instructions**: Calculate deviations, trace root assumptions, and summarize lessons
- **Verification Assertions**: At least 1 actionable system improvement is logged
- **Exit Requirements**: Approved post-mortem document
