# Negotiation Design Capability Contract

## Core Purpose
Design transaction incentives, structure agreements, and evaluate trade-offs.

## Quality Gates
- **Entry Preconditions**: List of stakeholders and proposed transaction values
- **Required Context**: Constitution_Quality and legal compliance summaries
- **Execution Instructions**: Analyze conflict points, calculate trade-offs, and draft balanced terms
- **Verification Assertions**: Agreement model covers all high-priority stakeholder needs
- **Exit Requirements**: Agreement structure specification
