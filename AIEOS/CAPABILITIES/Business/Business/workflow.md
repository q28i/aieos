# Business Strategy Capability Contract

## Core Purpose
Analyze market fit, define business logic requirements, and map user flows.

## Quality Gates
- **Entry Preconditions**: Market request guidelines and user profile summaries
- **Required Context**: Constitution_Documentation
- **Execution Instructions**: Decompose needs into features and outline value statements
- **Verification Assertions**: Each requirement is traced back to a verified market need
- **Exit Requirements**: Business requirements document
