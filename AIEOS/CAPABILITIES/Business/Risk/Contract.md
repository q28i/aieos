# Risk Management Capability Contract

## Core Purpose
Identify operational hazards, set mitigation plans, and calculate downside exposures.

## Quality Gates
- **Entry Preconditions**: Subsystem specifications and operational parameters
- **Required Context**: Constitution_Security and hazard databases
- **Execution Instructions**: Evaluate hazard probabilities, calculate downside values, and write response plans
- **Verification Assertions**: All identified risks have an assigned mitigation owner
- **Exit Requirements**: Verified risk register document
