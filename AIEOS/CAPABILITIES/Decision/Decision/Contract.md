# Decision Tracing Capability Contract

## Core Purpose
Document engineering choices, state evidence, evaluate tradeoffs, and assign reviews.

## Quality Gates
- **Entry Preconditions**: An architectural dilemma or proposed design change
- **Required Context**: Constitution_Documentation and system constraints list
- **Execution Instructions**: Examine tradeoffs, score complexity, and detail future risk parameters
- **Verification Assertions**: Verify evidence reports exist for performance/cost claims
- **Exit Requirements**: A structured ADR entry added to the project logs
