Model unit economics, calculate budget paths, and track capital efficiency.

- **Entry Preconditions**: Resource usage costs and transaction volumes
- **Required Context**: Constitution_Research and accounting guidelines
- **Execution Instructions**: Compute margins, calculate burn rate projections, and plot cost trajectories
- **Verification Assertions**: Budget assertions align with historical expense datasets
- **Exit Requirements**: Unit economics analysis sheet
